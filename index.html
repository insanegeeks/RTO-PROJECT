Welcome ....

The Transport Department of Goverment of Himachal Pradesh is entrusted with the responsibility of providing an efficient public transportation system, control of vehicular pollution, registration of vehicles in Himachal Pradesh, issuance of Driving licences, issuance of various permits, collection of road taxes. The department also entrusted in policy-making, co-ordination, implementation, monitoring and regulatory functions of all the Transport related aspects of Himachal Pradesh.
