Citizen Charter - Licence

 
Licence Registration Permit
Sr. NoService FormDocumentsTargeted/
Responsetime
1Learner's Licence Form 2
a. Copy of Valid Age Proof (Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate etc.)
b. Copy of Valid Address Proof (Ration Card, Electricity / Telephone Bill etc.)
c. Form 1 (Self declaration as to the physical fitness)
d. Form 1A in case of Transport Vehicle Licence
e. Two Recent Passport size Photographs
Same Day
2Driving Licence Form 4
a. Valid Learner's Licence for the relevant class, which is older than 30 days
b. Three Recent Passport size Photographs
Sent by Speed Post to be Delivered within 3 Days.
3Renewal of Driving Licence Form 9
a. Original Driving Licence
b. Form 1 (Self declaration as to the physical fitness)
c. Form 1A in case of Transport Vehicle Licence
d. Two Recent Passport size Photographs
Sent by Speed Post to be Delivered within 3 Days.
4Duplicate Copy of Licence Form L.L.D
a. Licence Zerox Copy (If Not available then get Licence Information on plain paper From RTO Office sign by Clerk)
Sent by Speed Post to be Delivered within 3 Days.
5Change of Address in Driving Licence Application on Plain Paper
a. Original Driving License
b. Address Proof
Sent by Speed Post to be Delivered within 3 Days.
6Endorsement in Driving Licence (Addition Of Another Class Of Vehicle) Form 8
a. Original Driving License
b. Learner's License of the class for which the, Applicant wants to get the Endorsement
c. Form 1 (Self declaration as to the physical fitness)
d. Form 1A in case of Transport Vehicle Licence
e. Applicants for Transport Vehicle Licence should enclose a Training Certificate in Form 5 issued by a recognized driving school.
 Sent by Speed Post to be Delivered within 3 Days.
7PSV Badge (Same Process as Addition of Another Class of Vehicle)Form 8
a. Original Driving License
b. Learner's License of relevant Badge
c. Form 1 (Self declaration as to the physical fitness)
d. Form 1A
e. Training Certificate in Form 5 issued by a recognized driving school.
f. Autorisation Form T.V.A for grant to drive Transport Vehicle
g. Character/Bonafied Certificate issued by Police station
 Sent by Speed Post to be Delivered within 3 Days.
8Issue Of International Driving PermitApplication on Plain Paper
a. Copy of Valid Driving Licence
b. Copy of Valid Air Ticket
c. Copy of Valid Passport
d. Copy of Valid Visa
e. Five passport size Photograph
Same Day
9Conductor Licence --
a.Mark sheet of 10th Pass or relevant Exam
b.Character Certificate from Polis Station
c.First Aid Certificate
d.Two Recent Passport size Photograph
e.Minimum Age Required 18 Year
Sent by Speed Post to be Delivered within 3 Days.
